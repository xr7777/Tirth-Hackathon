Compassion Connects Us All

Tirth is a compassionate and community-driven platform built to bridge the gap between urgent needs and timely help — for both animals and humans. Whether it's reporting an injured or distressed animal in your neighborhood, locating a lost pet, or connecting with nearby NGOs for rescue operations, Tirth ensures that help is just a few clicks away. For humans, we facilitate blood donation by connecting donors and recipients in real-time, saving precious lives when every second counts. You can also explore opportunities to adopt a loving animal companion and give them the forever home they deserve. Join Tirth and be a part of a network that truly cares — for all lives, big and small.
